Zeonix-G

COMPOSITION:

Nutrient Amount
SiO₂ 62-65%
Fe₂O₃ 2-3%
MgO 2-5%
Al₂O₃ 18-22%
CaO 15-18%
Na₂O 1-2%

Benifits

  • Zeonix-G having a unique molecular pore structure absorbs toxic gases like Ammonia (NH₃), Hydrogen Sulphide (H₂S) and other gases from pond water.
  • It has high 'Cation Exchange Capacity' develops a healthy environment for the shrimps and fish to grow.
  • Zeonix-G helps increase the amount of Dissolved Oxygen (DO) in the pond water.
  • Reduces foul odour from the pond bottom and helps to increase plankton growth.

DOSAGE:

Pond Preparation: Zeonix-G can be sprinkled evenly 5-10kg per Acre.

PACKING:

Presentation: 20 Kg Bag & 10 Kg Bucket
