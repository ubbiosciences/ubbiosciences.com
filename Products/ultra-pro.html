ULTRA PRO-L

COMPOSITION:

Calcium, Sodium, Copper, Manganese, Cobalt, Potassium, Silicon, Phosphorus, Selenium, Ferrous, Zinc, Iodine, Chlorides, Vitamins etc.

BENEFITS:

  • Helps in easy moulting
  • Prevents loose shell
  • Prevents soft shell
  • Prevents body cramps
  • Prevents white muscle disease
  • Helps better growth & body weight

DOSAGE:

2 Ltr. Per Acre 10 ml per 1 kg of Feed.

For Hatcheries:

  • Maturation Tank: 10 ppm
  • Larval Tanks: 2 ppm
  • PL Tanks: 5 ppm

As advised by aqua consultant.

PRESENTATION:

1 Liter
